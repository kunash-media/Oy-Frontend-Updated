Frequently Asked Questions

How do I clean my jewellery?

For most pieces, mix warm water with mild dish soap. Soak for 15-20 minutes, then gently brush with a soft toothbrush. Rinse and pat dry with a lint-free cloth. Avoid harsh chemicals.

What's your ring resizing policy?

We offer one complimentary resizing within 30 days of purchase. After that, resizing services start at $50 depending on the metal and design complexity.

Do you offer engraving?

Yes! We provide hand engraving on most pieces. Standard engraving starts at $25, with custom designs priced individually. Allow 3-5 additional business days for engraved items.
