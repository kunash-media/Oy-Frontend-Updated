What is imitation jewellery?

Imitation jewellery, also known as artificial or fashion jewellery, is designed to mimic the look of fine jewellery made from precious metals and gemstones. It uses a variety of materials like base metals (brass, copper, alloy, aluminium), plated metals (gold-plated, silver-plated), and simulated stones (cubic zirconia, glass, acrylic, resin) to offer stylish and affordable alternatives.

What materials are used in your jewellery?

Our jewellery is crafted using a range of high-quality materials, including:
* Base Metals: Brass, Copper, Alloy, Aluminium
* Plating: Gold plating, Silver plating, Rhodium plating
* Stones: High-quality Cubic Zirconia (CZ), glass, acrylic, resin, and various beads.
* Other: Enamel, pearls (faux).
We ensure our pieces are lead-free and nickel-free to minimize skin sensitivities.

Is your jewellery hypoallergenic?

While we strive to use materials that are well-tolerated, some individuals may have sensitivities to certain metals. We primarily use lead-free and nickel-free alloys to minimize allergic reactions. If you have extreme sensitivities, we recommend exercising caution or consulting with us before purchasing.

Will the colour of the jewellery fade or tarnish?

Imitation jewellery is designed for fashion and affordability, and while our pieces are made with quality plating, they can fade or tarnish over time with improper care or prolonged exposure to certain elements. Please refer to our "Jewellery Care" section for tips on how to extend the life of your pieces.

How do I care for my imitation jewellery?

Keep your imitation jewellery looking its best and extend its lifespan, we recommend the following:
Avoid Contact with Liquids: Remove your jewellery before washing hands, showering, swimming, or exercising. Water, sweat, and humidity can cause tarnishing.
Apply Products First: Put on your jewellery after applying perfumes, lotions, hairsprays, and other cosmetics. These chemicals can damage the plating.
Clean Gently: After each wear, gently wipe your jewellery with a soft, dry cloth (like a microfiber cloth) to remove any oils or dirt.
Proper Storage: Store each piece separately in an airtight bag or a jewellery box with individual compartments to prevent scratches and exposure to air and moisture. Keep it in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.
Avoid Harsh Chemicals: Never use harsh chemical cleaners, abrasives, or ultrasonic cleaners on your imitation jewellery as they can strip the plating.

Can I wear my jewellery every day?

While our jewellery is designed to be durable for everyday wear, continuous exposure to chemicals, sweat, and friction can accelerate wear and tear. We recommend rotating your pieces and following our care instructions to maintain their quality.
